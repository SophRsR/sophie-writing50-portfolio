Writing 50: Critical Reading and Rhetoric
Dr. Margaret Speer · Spring 2026

Hi! My name is Sophie Ren and I'm a first-year Business Administration + Computer Science student at UCI, with rich experience in journalism, photography, video production… and I'm still expanding my skill set!

Although I've been constantly exploring different subject areas, everything I've done is centered around a single purpose: I want to build technologies that truly serve humanity.

I created this portfolio as a collection of what I learned from my Writing 50 course. Our class focused on an interesting theme — medical horror — which reveals the dark side of human nature through the scope of disastrous events. I really enjoyed this writing process, and I hope you enjoy reading my work as well!
